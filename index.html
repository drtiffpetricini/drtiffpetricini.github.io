Course Description

This course introduces the concepts and skills of "public humanities" with a focus on digital media. We’ll work together to explore how public humanities projects can address pressing social issues, foster empathy, and build bridges between communities. This course includes hands-on training in documentary film, podcasting, and web publishing.

Our Goals and Learning Objectives

  • Critically analyze media’s role in shaping social justice narratives.
  • Engage with digital media tools to foster dialogue on social issues.
  • Explore public humanities and social justice through hands-on projects.
  • Evaluate the ethics of media advocacy.
  • Understand the relationships between media ecology, social justice, and public humanities.
  • Critically assess the role of AI in creative projects.
