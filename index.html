Tiffany Petricini

Associate Teaching Professor of Communication at Penn State Erie

Specialties: Media Ecology, Phenomenology, Interpersonal Communication

About Me

Currently, I am an Associate Teaching Professor in the field of Communication Studies at Penn State Erie in Erie, Pennsylvania. Some of my favorite courses are Interpersonal Communication, Group Communication, and Mass Media and Society. I try to facilitate and encourage a joy for learning, which is my personal passion.

Publications & Research

While teaching is my primary focus, I am very active in the scholarly community and have published several articles and a book, "Friendship and Technology" with Routledge.

Speaking Engagements

I have been an invited speaker on the international radio program Spark on CBC Radio One and was the first external speaker to ever be invited to the SUNY Plattsburgh Ethics Institute. I also serve as the social media expert for NBC affiliate WFMJ 21 News.

Service & Leadership

I participate in various types of service for the campus, community, university, and profession and hold several university leadership roles. I am co-chair of the Joint Standing Committee on Responsible Use of Artificial Intelligence for Penn State University and represent my campus as a senator for the Pennsylvania State University Faculty Senate.
