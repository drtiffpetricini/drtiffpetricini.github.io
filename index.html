Welcome

Welcome to my professional website! Currently, I am an Associate Teaching Professor of Communication studies at Penn State Erie, The Behrend Campus in Erie, Pennsylvania. My personal passion is learning, and I try to encourage a joy of learning in all of my interactions with others. While teaching is my primary focus, I am active in the scholarly community and have published several articles and my book, "Friendship and Technology" with Routledge. I have been an invited speaker at several events and internationally recognized. I am also the social media expert for NBC-affiliate WFMJ 21 News. Here, you can explore my work as a scholar and educator in Communication Studies. Please feel free to browse my publications, media appearances, and projects, or learn more about my teaching philosophy and class resources.
