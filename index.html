Course Overview

This course introduces students to the field of public humanities with a focus on digital media tools. Together, we'll explore how public humanities work can address social issues and foster empathy, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Learning Objectives

  • Critically analyze the role of media in shaping social justice narratives.
  • Engage with digital media tools to create content for public dialogue.
  • Explore the ethics of using media for advocacy and public engagement.
  • Develop and apply hands-on skills in podcasting, documentary, and web publishing.
