Can I use generative AI like ChatGPT or Dall-E for any of Dr. P's courses?

You may only use generative AIs in ways we discuss in class or if you have explicit permission sought before creating a deliverable or completing an assessment.

Can I use generative AI in all my courses?

No. Different faculty have different policies, and they may vary by course. Always confirm with your instructor for each course.

What exactly is plagiarism anyway?

Plagiarism involves using others' work without proper acknowledgment. I recommend earning your Academic Integrity Certificate to better understand it.

Dr. P.'s Three Rules about Using AI

  1. Be truthful about your use of AI tools.
  2. Use it to support, not replace your work.
  3. Take chances creatively and ethically.

Artificial Intelligence Guidelines

Generative AI tools have limitations. Consider:

  • AI lacks critical reasoning and audience awareness.
  • AI may produce false data or unsupported assertions.
  • AI-generated paraphrasing can result in mosaic plagiarism.
