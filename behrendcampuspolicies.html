Disability and Learning Differences Support

Penn State welcomes students with disabilities into the University’s educational programs. Every Penn State campus has an office for students with disabilities. Visit the Student Disability Resources website for contact information and further details.

To receive accommodations, contact the disability services office at your campus, complete an intake interview, and provide documentation: see documentation guidelines. Share your accommodation letter with instructors early each semester.

Personal Counseling Services

Many students face personal challenges or psychological needs that may interfere with their academic progress or well-being. Confidential services are available, including individual and group counseling, crisis intervention, and mental health screenings. Contact Penn State Behrend Personal Counseling or use these resources:

  • Penn State Crisis Line (24/7): 877-229-6400
  • Crisis Text Line (24/7): Text LIONS to 741741

Free Food Pantry

Penn State Behrend offers a food pantry service to support students in need. Please contact the Student Affairs Office for more details.

Learning Resource Center: Free Tutoring

Tutoring can help clarify concepts, provide advice on study methods, and prepare for exams. Schedule an appointment at tutorapp.bd.psu.edu or visit the Learning Resource Center at 203 Lilley Library. Contact: 814-898-6140, behrend.psu.edu/lrc.

Starfish Early Alert System

The Starfish system is available to support students by connecting them with resources and helping manage academic success.

Diversity Statement and Reporting Bias

We support an equitable environment for all, valuing diversity and inclusion. Acts of discrimination or intolerance are not tolerated and can be reported through Educational Equity.

Title IX

Penn State fosters a harassment-free environment as mandated by Title IX. For more information or to report incidents, contact the Title IX Coordinator or at Penn State Behrend, contact Kelly Shrout at kaw148@psu.edu or call (814) 898-6111.

Technology Help and IT Support

Penn State Behrend’s IT Helpdesk provides technology support to students. Contact IT Support for assistance with devices, connectivity, and online resources.

Career Services

Career Services offers support with resumes, cover letters, job searches, interview prep, and more. Visit their office at Reed 125 during drop-in hours Monday-Friday, 12:00-4:00 p.m., schedule an appointment through Starfish, or call 814-898-6164.

Inclement Weather Procedures

During extreme weather, check for class updates via:

  • Text alerts if registered for PSUAlert.
  • Email updates to your Penn State address.
  • Announcements on behrend.psu.edu.
  • Recorded message at the college’s main number: 814-898-6000.

For exam or assignment adjustments, I will communicate updates via Canvas as early as possible.
